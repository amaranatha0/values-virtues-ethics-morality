Values

Values are core beliefs or standards that guide behavior and decision-making. They are deeply held principles that reflect what is important to individuals and societies. Examples include honesty, integrity, and respect.

Virtues

Virtues are positive traits or qualities deemed to be morally good. They represent good character and moral excellence. Examples include courage, kindness, and patience.

Ethics

Ethics are moral principles that govern a person's behavior or the conducting of an activity. They provide a framework for determining what is right and wrong. Examples include professional ethics, business ethics, and medical ethics.

Morality

Morality is a system of principles and values concerning the distinction between right and wrong or good and bad behavior. It often derives from societal norms, cultural practices, and religious beliefs. Examples include moral codes, ethical conduct, and virtuous living.
